Section 01

Introduction

Welcome to HIM.clothiers. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make purchases from our store.

Our Promise: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Your information is used solely to provide you with the best shopping experience and to improve our services.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our website or services.

Section 02

Information We Collect

We collect several types of information from and about users of our website, including:

Personal Info

Name, email address, phone number, shipping and billing addresses

Purchase Data

Order history, product preferences, cart items, wishlist items

Payment Info

Payment method details (processed securely by our payment partners)

Device Info

IP address, browser type, device type, operating system

  • Information you provide by filling in forms on our website
  • Records and copies of your correspondence with us

  • Details of transactions you carry out through our website
  • Your search queries on our website
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect about you for various purposes, including:

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders, including sending confirmation emails and delivery updates
  • Account Management: To manage your account, including verifying your identity and maintaining your preferences
  • Personalization: To personalize your shopping experience and recommend products you may like
  • Communication: To send you promotional materials, newsletters, and special offers (with your consent)
  • Improvement: To improve our website, products, and customer service
  • Security: To protect our website, business, and users from fraud and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Marketing Communications: You can opt-out of marketing emails at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link at the bottom of our emails or by contacting us directly.

Section 04

Data Sharing & Disclosure

We may disclose personal information that we collect or you provide as described in this privacy policy:

  • Service Providers: To contractors, service providers, and other third parties we use to support our business (payment processors, shipping companies, email service providers)
  • Business Transfers: In connection with any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with any court order, law, or legal process, including to respond to any government or regulatory request
  • Protection: To enforce our terms of service and protect the rights, property, or safety of HIM.clothiers, our customers, or others

Important: We require all third-party service providers to respect the security of your personal data and to treat it in accordance with the law. We do not allow them to use your personal data for their own purposes.

Section 05

Security Measures

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information:

SSL Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL technology

Secure Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers with restricted access and regular security audits

Access Control

Only authorized personnel can access your personal data, and only when necessary

Regular Updates

We continuously update our security practices to protect against new threats

Despite our efforts, no security measures are perfect or impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the security of your personal information, but we continuously work to protect it.

Section 06

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate
  • Erasure: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions
  • Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data
  • Data Portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data we have collected to another organization
  • Object to Processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data
  • Withdraw Consent: You have the right to withdraw consent where we are relying on consent to process your personal data

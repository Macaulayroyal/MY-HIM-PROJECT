Orders & Purchases

Placing an order is easy:

  • Browse our collection and select your desired items
  • Choose your size and color preferences
  • Click "Add to Cart" and proceed to checkout
  • Fill in your delivery details and complete payment
  • You'll receive an order confirmation via email

You can track your order in multiple ways:

  • Check your Orders page for real-time status updates
  • Click on any order to see detailed tracking information
  • You'll receive SMS and email updates at each stage
  • Contact our support team with your order ID for assistance

Order modifications depend on the status:

  • Pending Payment: You can cancel and reorder
  • Paid/Processing: Contact us immediately for changes
  • Shipped: Cannot be modified, but can be returned after delivery

Returns & Exchanges

Our return policy:

  • Timeframe: 7 days from delivery date
  • Condition: Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached
  • Exceptions: Underwear, socks, and customized items cannot be returned
  • Process: Initiate return through your orders page or contact support

To return an item:

  1. Go to your Orders page and select the order
  2. Click "Request Return" and select the item(s)
  3. Choose reason for return and preferred resolution (refund/exchange)
  4. Package the item securely in original packaging
  5. Our courier will pick up within 48 hours

Yes! Size exchanges are free within the 7-day window, subject to availability. If your size is out of stock, you can choose to:

  • Wait for restock (we'll notify you)
  • Select a different item of same value
  • Receive store credit
  • Get a refund to original payment method

Products & Sizing

Finding your perfect fit:

  • Check our detailed Size Guide with measurements
  • Measure your chest, waist, and hips in inches
  • Compare with our size chart on each product page
  • When in doubt, size up for a relaxed fit or down for fitted
  • Read customer reviews for fit feedback

Care instructions by material:

  • Cotton: Machine wash cold, tumble dry low, iron medium heat
  • Polyester blends: Machine wash cold, do not bleach, hang dry
  • Wool: Dry clean only or hand wash cold
  • Denim: Wash inside out, avoid frequent washing

Always check the care label inside your garment.
